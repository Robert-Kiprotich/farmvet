EMERGENCY CARE

Animal Emergency Care refers to the immediate medical attention provided to animals experiencing critical or life-threatening health conditions. Similar to emergency services for humans, animal emergency care aims to stabilize the animal's condition, alleviate pain, and prevent further harm until a comprehensive treatment plan can be implemented.

LIVESTOCK EXAMINATION

Livestock Examination refers to the systematic evaluation of animals to assess their health, diagnose diseases, and identify conditions that may affect their productivity or well-being. This process is critical for veterinarians, livestock managers, and farmers to ensure the health and productivity of their herds.
